# References

## Kernel Recursive Least Squares (KRLS) and ALD

**The Kernel Recursive Least-Squares Algorithm**
Yaakov Engel, Shie Mannor, Ron Meir
*IEEE Transactions on Signal Processing*, vol. 52, no. 8, pp. 2275–2285, August 2004
- Introduces KRLS and the Approximate Linear Dependency (ALD) sparsification criterion
- https://doi.org/10.1109/TSP.2004.830985
- https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/1315946/

---

## Sliding-Window Kernel RLS (SW-KRLS)

**A Sliding-Window Kernel RLS Algorithm and Its Application to Nonlinear Channel Identification**
S. Van Vaerenbergh, J. Vía, I. Santamaría
*ICASSP 2006*, IEEE International Conference on Acoustics, Speech and Signal Processing, vol. 5, pp. 789–792
- Original sliding-window KRLS with age-based eviction and Sherman-Morrison downdate
- https://doi.org/10.1109/ICASSP.2006.1661394
- https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/1661394/

---

## Improved SW-KRLS with ALD and Forgetting Factor

**Improved Sliding Window Kernel RLS Algorithm for Identification of Time-Varying Nonlinear Systems**
Xinyu Guo, Menghua Jiang, Ying Gao, Shifeng Ou, Jindong Xu, Zhuoran Cai
*Advanced Hybrid Information Processing* (ADHIP 2021), Lecture Notes of the Institute for Computer Sciences,
Social Informatics and Telecommunications Engineering (LNICST), vol. 422, pp. 203–220, Springer, 2022
- Combines sliding window, ALD novelty detection, and forgetting factor
- https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-030-94554-1_18
- https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-3-030-94554-1_18
